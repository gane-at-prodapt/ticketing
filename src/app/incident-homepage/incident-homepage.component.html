INCIDENTS

Incident, also known as tickets, are raised when a network or a component is not working and you need help fixing it. After a ticket is raised, The intial state of the ticket is 'open' it means the issue is with us and we will be taking action. Your ticket will be directed to the particular group. The ticked can be ignored or resolved. When the ticket is 'resolved' it means a Support Analyst resolved your ticket by providing a rootcause and solution. After the the ticket is resolved, it will send to the Super Admin and Network Admin, they can analyze the solution provided by support analyst and if it is correct, they will close the ticket.
